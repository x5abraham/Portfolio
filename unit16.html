Unit 16
Automated Computer Systems

In this unit, we learnt to investigate the features and uses of websites by exploring what they are and how their integrated components and applications interact with each other.

We also learnt how to design, develop and test a website for a brief. Once this was completed we then reviewed our website, and obtained feedback from others.

Assignment 1

Before we developed our website for Invasion Corp, we had been asked first to investigate two websites. We had to explain the uses of the two websites and the intended purpose of their features, aswell as discuss the strengths and weaknesses of both websites.
